# Product Overview

MotoCheck is a Nigerian vehicle history and import duty calculation platform. Users enter a 17-character VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) to receive an instant comprehensive report containing:

- Full vehicle specifications from NHTSA database
- Line-by-line Nigerian import duty calculations using NCS valuation tables
- Active safety recalls and OEM notices
- Manufacturing details and market compliance information
- CBN exchange rate-based cost estimates

The platform charges ₦2,500 per report (flat fee) and delivers PDF/DOCX reports via email within 60 seconds of payment confirmation. Payment processing is handled through Paystack (cards, bank transfers, USSD).

Target users are Nigerian vehicle importers, car dealers, fleet managers, and private buyers who need accurate duty estimates and vehicle verification before purchasing vehicles from overseas markets.

The platform also includes a Telegram bot interface for VIN lookups and report purchases.
